About

Three Days of Inspiration!

28 - 31 January 2016, Bursa, TR

This year's conference will be a boost for the Let's Do It! World Cleanup 2018 campaign. It will start a transformation in global waste management and how waste is perceived in society. This is the only place to be that current time and your country can't miss the final decision making! We invite civic activists and opinion leaders from different countries around the world to come and brainstorm about the World Cleanup 2018.

Our Aim

We will organise and carry out a global cleanup that takes place on one day in 2018, involving 380 million which is approximately 5% of the World's population. This conference will help participants get onto the same page and provide real inspiration.

The Vision

Our vision is simple - a clean world.
For one day, the world will be focused on one cause with the most amazing results - a clean, waste-free and healthy world, and a renewed belief that together we can solve global problems. It will be the largest scale initiative in the history of humankind!

Working Groups

The pre-registration for the working groups will be announced to the selected participants of the conference after the main registration has ended. The working groups will be conducted in two parallel sessions and they will be performed by outstanding people.

Getting Here

Air Travel

We strongly advise you to land at one of Istanbul's airports, either Ataturk International Airport(IST) or Sabiha Gokcen International Airport(SAW). Those are the busiest airports which have the most frequent flights from all over the World.

By Bus

At a given time, there will be city shuttles which bring you from the Istanbul airports to coach stations. After you arrive the coach station, wide range of the bus companies you may choose to come to Bursa. Other details are explained in the information package.

Travel Notes

At the end of January, Bursa will be in Winter time which means it is cold. Please note, bring warm garments. Also, we remind you to check all details on the information package to avoid any unpleasantness.
